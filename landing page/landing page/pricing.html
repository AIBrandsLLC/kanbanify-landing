Pricing FAQ

Questions before you start?

Is the Free plan really free forever?

Yes. Up to 5 boards and unlimited members, with no time limit and no credit card. Upgrade only when you need unlimited boards, group calls, or advanced reporting.

How does per-user billing work?

You're billed only for active members in your workspace. Add or remove seats anytime — yearly plans are prorated, and there are no surprise overage fees.

What happens when my trial ends?

Nothing breaks. If you don't upgrade, your workspace moves to the Free plan automatically — your data stays intact and you keep up to 5 boards.

Can I switch plans or cancel later?

Anytime, from your billing settings. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your current cycle. No lock-in.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?

Yes — reach out to our team and we'll set you up with eligible pricing.
